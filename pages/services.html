Innovative MEP Design & Sustainable Engineering

Delivering precision-driven Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing solutions tailored for complex infrastructure and modern developments.

Mechanical (HVAC) Engineering

Our HVAC solutions are engineered to optimize thermal comfort and indoor air quality while significantly reducing energy consumption. We provide end-to-end design services that ensure your building’s climate control systems are efficient, reliable, and compliant with international standards.

  • Precision Heat Load Calculations: Ensuring optimal equipment sizing for efficiency.
  • Advanced Duct Layout & Sizing: Designed for balanced airflow and minimal noise.
  • Chilled Water Pipe Sizing & Design: High-performance cooling network architecture.
  • Ventilation & Exhaust Systems: Specialized solutions for fresh air intake and industrial exhaust.

Electrical & Power Engineering

We design robust and scalable electrical systems focused on safety, efficiency, and uninterrupted power distribution. Our designs adhere to the latest electrical codes to ensure long-term reliability for residential and commercial infrastructures.

  • Comprehensive Lighting & Power Layouts: Optimized for energy saving and functionality.
  • Detailed Single Line Diagrams (SLD): Clear architecture for complex power distribution.
  • Precise Cable Sizing & Scheduling: Preventing voltage drops and ensuring safety.
  • Earthing & Lightning Protection: Advanced safety systems to protect equipment and life.

Public Health Engineering (Plumbing) & Fire Safety

Our engineering approach integrates sustainable water management with life-saving fire protection systems. We develop sophisticated hydraulic models that ensure efficient water supply, drainage, and rapid-response fire suppression.

  • Water Supply & Drainage Layouts: Designed for optimal pressure and hygiene.
  • Specialized Septic Tank & STP Design: Environmentally conscious waste management.
  • Fire Sprinkler & Hydrant Systems: Fully compliant with NFPA and local safety standards.
  • Advanced Fire Alarm & Detection: High-sensitivity systems for early risk mitigation.
